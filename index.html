The ellipse represents the opening of the futures cone. The inner ellipse marks the boundary of futures framed as more inevitable; the outer zone contains futures framed as more contingent. Oval span reflects the degree of contestation within the discourse. Imaginaries are not fixed at specific positions; their placement reflects tendencies observed in the data.
